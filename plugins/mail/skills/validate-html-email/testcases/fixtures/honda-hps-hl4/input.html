Honda has redesigned the 2026 Pioneer 1000 from the ground up, with three new models to choose from: the three-seat Pioneer 1000 Deluxe, the five-seat Pioneer 1000-5 Deluxe, and the sealed-cab Pioneer 1000 Elite coming later this year. However you work and wherever you roam, there’s a new Pioneer 1000 built to perfectly fit your life.
Work easy. Play hard.
 
Selectable ride modes, a redesigned interior, and features like cruise control help make the new Deluxe models ready for the workday and the weekend. Choose the three-person Pioneer 1000 Deluxe when it’s all business or go with the five-person Pioneer 1000-5 and its QuickFlip seating for more passengers and more stories.
 
Live that Elite life
 
Meet the all-new Pioneer 1000 Elite — Honda’s first side-by-side with a sealed cab and standard heating and air conditioning. It’s coming soon, bringing decades of automotive experience that’s designed to set a new standard for side-by-side comfort and capability. If winter workdays or summer heat are part of your routine, it’ll surely be worth the wait.
